FINDING

THE EXTINCT

NEW ZEALAND BIRD

Ourselves, we are writing a song.
It's based on a journey that lasts very long,
to find the extinct takahe bird,
while trying to rhyme the final word.

It starts with an explorer who falls in love,
with a colourful bird from the land above.
He searched and searched throughout the land,
but the lost bird is far from hand.

The bird stands tall at 20 inches high,
with 3-foot wings but unable to fly.
With lots of bright colours he looks pretty too;
green, white, purple, pink, red, black and blue.

Thought to be lost by all of the rest,
the exploroer would find him 'cus he is the best.
In New Zealand the bird lives life on the other side,
but with thanks to the explorer he is still alive.

Dr. Orbell
